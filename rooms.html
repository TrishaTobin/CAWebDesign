All rooms in Seahaven offer the following to its guests:

  • luxuriously furnished
  • private bathrooms with full size baths
  • separate walk-in rain showers
  • walnut wooden floors
  • central heating
  • iron and ironing board
  • hair dryer
  • 32" to 40" flat TV
  • tea/coffee making facilites
  • all rooms are non smoking

The regular check-in times are 12pm - 6pm.

Please make Seahaven aware if you plan to arrive outside of these hours.

Breakfast is served from 7.30am-10am weekdays and 7am-10am at weekends.

Breakfast at Seahaven is a wonderful experience - all ingredients are sourced locally and prepared fresh each morning, including our breads. Full Breakfast is available. Vegetarians and vegans are especially welcome.
