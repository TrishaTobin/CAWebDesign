About Us

Seahaven B&B has been the home to Tom Ryan and his family for over 20 years. Seahaven is situated in the beautiful village of Malahide overlooking Dublin Bay. All Seahaven's guests are guaranteed to experience spectactular seaviews during their stay.

Tom loves to welcome guests into the Ryan home and each guest receives a friendly and genuine welcome. Sightseeing tours can also be arranged to Malahide Castle, Howth or bus tours of Dublin City i.e. Viking tours or visits to Trinity Collge Dublin. See our FAQ page for more information.
